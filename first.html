八一起义纪念馆

南昌八一起义纪念馆位于南昌市中山路380号。1956年开始筹建，1959年正式对外开放，1961年被国务院公布为全国重点文物保护单位。
Located at # 380 Zhongshan Road, Nanchang, Nanchang August 1st Uprising Memorial Hall laid its first brick in 1956 and was opened to the public in 1959 and has been in the list of the "National key Culture Relic Protection Sites" since 1961.
所辖五处革命旧址一一八一起义总指挥部旧址、叶挺指挥部旧址、朱德旧居、朱德军官教育团旧址、贺龙指挥部旧址均为全国重点文物保护单位。
It is in charge of another five culture relic protection sites, namely, the Former Headquarters of the August 1st Uprising, the Former Headquarters of Ye Ting's Troops, the Former Residence of Zhu De, the Former Site of the Officers' Training Corps of the 3rd Army Group of the National Revolutionary Army and the Former Headquarters of He Long's Troops, which are all in the list too.

武装反抗国民党反动派的第一枪

1927年8月1日凌晨2时，在以周恩来为书记的中共前敌委员会的领导下，贺龙、叶挺、朱德、刘伯承等率领党掌握和影响的军队2万余人在南昌举行起义，打响了武装反抗国民党反动派的第一枪，标志着中国共产党独立领导革命战争、创建人民军队和武装夺取政权的开始。
On August 1,1927,at 2 A.M ,Under the leadership of Zhou Enlai, General Secretary of the Front Committee of the Communist Party of China, He Long, Ye Ting, Zhu De, Liu Bocheng and other 20 thousand soldiers for the Uprising to shoot the(the CPC) first bullet to fight against the Kuomintang reactionaries, which signified the beginning of the independent military revolution, the buildup of the people's army, as well as the military way of stepping into power led by the CPC.

展馆呈现方式

整个展馆以多样的呈现方式，如丰富多彩的照片、珍贵的文物、沙盘幻影剧场、多媒体魔墙区，全面生动地反映南昌起义的光辉历史，展现了在中国共产党领导下，人民军队由小到大，由弱到强，历尽千辛万苦，战胜千难万险，为实现民族独立、人民解放和国家富强、人民幸福建立了不朽的功勋!
The whole pavilion reflects the glorious history of Nanchang Uprising in a comprehensive and vivid way with various presentation methods, such as precious cultural relics, sandbox phantom theater sandtable projectors, and multimedia magic wall area, showing that under the leadership of the Communist Party of China, the people's army grew from small to big, from weak to strong, and overcame thousands of hardships and difficulties to establish immortal merits for achieving national independence, people's liberation and national prosperity and people's happiness!

“八一精神”

南昌起义铸就了伟大的“八一精神”，其主要内涵是信念坚定、听党指挥的革命精神，百折不挠、为民奋斗的献身精神，不畏强暴、敢为人先的斗争精神，独立自主、勇于创新的开拓精神，精诚团结、共同对敌的合作精神，是中国革命精神宝库的重要组成部分，是始终激励和鼓舞中国共产党人和人民军队战胜一切艰难险阻、不断夺取胜利的伟大精神力量。
The Nanchang Uprising has forged the great "August 1st Spirit", whose main connotation is the revolutionary spirit of firm faith and obedience to the command of the Party, the spirit of dedication and struggle for the people, the spirit of struggle in defiance of violence and daring to be the first, the pioneering spirit of independence and innovation, and the spirit of cooperation and unity against the enemy, which are the spirits of the Chinese Revolution. It is an important part of the treasury of the Chinese revolutionary spirit, and is the great spiritual force that always inspires and encourages the Chinese Communists and the People's Army to overcome all hardships and dangers, and to win victory continuously.

八一南昌起义革命故事

From the lessons of the defeat of the Revolution, the Chinese Communist Party began to realize the importance of independently mastering the revolutionary armed forces.
从大革命失败的教训中，中国共产党开始认识到独立掌握革命武装力量的重要性。
The bloody massacre policy of the Kuomintang reactionaries aroused great anger and strong resistance from the Chinese Communist Party and the revolutionary masses.
国民党反动派的血腥屠杀政策，激起了中国共产党和革命群众的极大愤怒和强烈反抗。
On the night of July 31, 1927, Zhu De held the enemy Third Army's 23rd and 24th regiments and regimental deputies at 32 Dashiyuan under the pretext of treating them, which strongly complemented the uprising battle.
1927年7月31日晚，朱德以请客为名，在大士院32号牵制了敌第三军第二十三、二十四团和团副，有力地配合了起义战斗。

八一南昌起义革命故事

At about 9:00 p.m. on the same day, a deputy battalion commander of the 20th Army defected to the enemy, and the situation was very critical. The CPC front committee immediately decided to advance the uprising by two hours.
同日晚9时许，第二十军中的一个副营长投敌告密，情况十分危急。中共前委当即决定:起义提前两小时。
At 2 a.m. of August 1st, under the leadership of He Long, Ye Ting, Zhu De, Liu Bocheng and the CPC Front Committee with Zhou Enlai as the secretary, the insurrectionary army launched an intensive attack as planned on the enemy defenders stationed in Nanchang. After four hours of fierce fighting, the insurrectionary army seized the city of Nanchang, accomplishing its first armed resistance against the Kuomintang reactionaries.
1927年8月1日凌晨2时，在以周恩来为书记的中共前敌委员会及贺龙、叶挺、朱德、刘伯承等领导下，起义部队按照预定的部署，向驻守南昌的敌军发起猛烈进攻，经过4个多小时的激烈战斗，占领南昌城，打响了武装反抗国民党反动派的第一枪。

八一南昌起义革命故事

In the course of the uprising The Fourth Regiment of the Twentieth Army of He Long's Department stormed the barracks of the enemy patrol, wiped out the enemy troops, took control of Niu Xing Station, and assumed the important task of guarding the Nanxun Railway and blocking the Ganjiang waterway.
在起义的过程中，贺龙部第二十军第四团冲进敌巡防队营房，全歼敌军，控制了牛行车站，担负起警戒南浔铁路、封锁赣江水道的重要任务。
According to the prior agreement between Zhou Enlai and Nie Rongzhen, as soon as the uprising started, a train was immediately sent from Nanchang to Mahuiling to inform the comrades of the 25th Division of the Fourth Army stationed there.
根据周恩来和聂荣臻的事先约定，起义一打响，立即从南昌发一列火车到马回岭，以告知驻扎在那里的第四军第二十五师的同志。

八一南昌起义革命故事

As soon as this train arrived, Nie Rongzhen and others immediately led the bulk of the 25th Division of the Fourth Army in the uprising at Mahuiling, marched to De'an Railway Station, and collected the guns of Zhang Fa-kui's guard battalion that had come to pursue and attack them.
等这一火车一到，聂荣臻等人立即率领第四军第二十五师大部在马回岭起义，行进至德安火车站，收缴了前来追击的张发奎卫队营的枪械。
The whole uprising wiped out more than 3,000 people enemies and captured more than 500 machine guns, 5,000 rifles and 700,000 rounds of ammunition.
整场起义歼灭了3000余人，缴获机枪500余挺，5000余支步枪，700000余发子弹。
