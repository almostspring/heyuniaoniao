忠诚誓言

  In the western suburbs of Nanchang City, Jiangxi Province, between the green pines and cypresses, rests the nine bones of a martyr who survived in the world, and that martyr is Fang Zhimin.
在江西省南昌市西郊的梅岭，在这青松翠柏之间，安放着一位烈士仅存于世上的九块遗骨，这位烈士就是方志敏。
He also wrote down the oath: "Communist - this is a very honorable term, and how proud I am to have joined the Communist Party and to be a Communist! From now on, everything I do, up to and including my life, will be given to the Party." From the first day he joined the Party, Fang Zhimin gave everything he had to the Party. Whether he was in good times or bad, he worked diligently for the Party and the people, and his ideals and beliefs never wavered.
他还记下誓言：“共产党员——这是一个极尊贵的名词，我加入了共产党，做了共产党员，我是如何地引以为荣呵！从此，我的一切，直至我的生命都交给党去了。”从入党的第一天起，方志敏就把自己的一切交给了党，无论个人身处顺境还是逆境，他都兢兢业业为党为人民工作，理想和信念从未动摇过。

忠贞实践

  At the end of January 1935, Fang was captured and imprisoned in Nanchang Nationalist Detention Center, where he resolutely refused to be persuaded to surrender, fulfilling his vow to " work hard until death, fight until death".
1935年1月底，陷于绝境的方志敏不幸被俘，囚于南昌国民党看守所，并严辞拒绝了国民党的劝降，实践了自己“努力到死，奋斗到死”的誓言。1935年8月，方志敏同志被秘密杀害于江西省南昌市下沙窝，时年36岁。
Fang also wrote in prison, "Waiting calmly for the moment, for the bullet or the knife!" He emphasized, "We must prepare slogans, and when we are about to be executed, we must shout loudly and with vigor to show our unyielding!" Life, for each person, comes only once. There is nothing more difficult than the choice between life and death. Fang Zhimin chose to die because he could not "abandon his original doctrinal beliefs and tear up his former history of struggle".
方志敏还在狱中写道：“心平气静地等候着那一刻儿的到来，等候着那一颗子弹，或是一刀！”他强调：“我们必须准备口号，临刑时，要高声的呼，用劲的呼，以表示我们的不屈！”生命，对于每一个人来说都只有一次。没有什么比生死抉择更为艰难。方志敏义无反顾地选择了死，是因为决不能“抛弃自己原来的主义信仰，撕毁自己从前的斗争历史”。
