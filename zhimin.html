志愿入党

  Fang Zhimin （August 21, 1899 - August 6, 1935）Formerly known as Yuanzhen, with the maiden name Zhengkwan, was known as Huisheng . He was a proletarian revolutionary, militarist, and outstanding leader of the peasant movement , and the founder of the revolutionary bases in northeast Jiangxi and Min-Zhe-Gan (namely Fujian, Zhejiang, Jiangxi Province) during the Agrarian Revolutionary War.
方志敏（1899年8月21日～1935年8月6日），原名远镇，乳名正鹄，号慧生 。江西上饶市弋阳九区漆工镇湖塘村人，无产阶级革命家、军事家、杰出的农民运动领袖，土地革命战争时期赣东北和闽浙赣革命根据地的创建人。
In July 1922, Fang Zhimin quit school and came to Shanghai, where he met revolutionary friends such as Zhao Shengnong and joined the Chinese Socialist Youth League. In September of the same year, he returned to Jiangxi, where he was responsible for promoting the Party's theory, uniting progressive youth and carrying out revolutionary activities. Two years later, Fang Zhimin joined the Communist Party of China, and he felt honored and proud.
1922年7月，方志敏退学来到上海，结识赵醒侬等革命挚友，并加入中国社会主义青年团。同年9月他返回江西，负责宣传党的理论、团结进步青年、开展革命活动。两年后，方志敏加入中国共产党，他倍感光荣和自豪。

慷慨赴死

  Fang was the chairman of the Soviet Government of Fujian, Zhejiang and Jiangxi Province, the political commissar of the Tenth Red Army and the chairman of the military and political committee of the Tenth Red Army Corps, as the main military and political leader of the base area.
方志敏先后担任闽浙赣省苏维埃政府主席，红十军政治委员、红十军团军政委员会主席等重要职务，作为根据地的军政主要领导。
On January 29, 1935, Fang Zhimin was unfortunately captured. The Kuomintang wanted to celebrate his capture by putting him on an armored car and a high platform, but Fang stood up with his head held high, without fear and with a strong spirit, which made the Kuomintang authorities very nervous, fearing that he would be rescued and taken away by the masses. In prison, Fang Zhimin was steadfast and unyielding. He used his pen as a gun and expressed his thoughts straightforwardly: We are communists, of course, we all hold an active and struggling outlook on life...... We live for the revolution and are willing to die for it!
1935年1月29日，方志敏不幸被俘。国民党妄想“示众”庆贺，将他押上铁甲车和高台，不料方志敏昂首挺立、毫无惧色、正气凛然，反倒让国民党当局十分紧张，生怕他被群众营救抢走。方志敏在狱中坚贞不屈，他以笔为枪、直抒胸臆：“我们是共产党员，当然都抱着积极奋斗的人生观……我们为革命而生，更愿为革命而死！”
